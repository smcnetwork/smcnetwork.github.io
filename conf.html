Current and past editions

The SMC Conference is a double-blind peer-reviewed international scientific conference around the core interdisciplinary topics of Sound and Music Computing.

The proceedings of SMC are registered with ISSN 2518-3672.

The next SMC conference will take place in Malaga.

The SMC Conferences are jointly supervised by:

  • AFIM (Association Française d'Informatique Musicale)
  • AIMI (Associazione Italiana di Informatica Musicale)
  • DEGEM (Deutsche Gesellschaft für Elektroakustische Musik)
  • HACI (Hellenic Association of Music Informatics)
  • SITEMU (Sociedad(e) Ibérica de TEcnología MUsical)
